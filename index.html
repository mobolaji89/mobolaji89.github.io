Mobolaji's Fishing Adventures

I love fishing the lakes, streams, and rivers of Colorado. There a many species of fish that inhabit the waters in Colorado. Some of the popular species for fishing include:

  • Largemouth Bass
  • Smallmouth Bass
  • Rainbow Trout
  • Brown Trout
  • Walleye
  • Yellow Perch
  • Crappie
  • Channel Catfish


I personally enjoy fishing for Walleye and Rainbow Trout in the spring and fall, and then both smallmouth and largemouth bass in the summer. Here are some beautiful pictures of these species.
